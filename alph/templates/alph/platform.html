Why do you need a trading platform?

A trading platform is computer software that online traders and investors can use to trade the global markets with a broker or other financial intermediary.

There are a range of trading platforms available, including online platforms which operate on desktop, or platforms which run on mobile apps.

Trading platforms can be simple order entry screens for beginners or they can be quite sophisticated user interfaces with comprehensive tool suites, including live quote streaming, news updates and chart analysis functionality for advanced and professional traders.
