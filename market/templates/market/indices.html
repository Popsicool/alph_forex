Why Trade Indices?

Indices are an ideal way to trade a basket of stocks and diversify your investment portfolio across different sectors. It is a convenient way to trade CFDs on indices and explore multiple opportunities at once without having to trade CFDs on each individual stock. By trading CFDs on an index, you trade several companies stocks and diversify your exposure. You can also trade the world markets as you can access indices from around the world such as the FTSE 100, Aussie 200 or the German DAX.
